Botnet — The Killer Web App

A botnet is a network of compromised devices (bots) controlled by an operator through command‑and‑control (C2). They power DDoS, credential stuffing, and spam campaigns.

Lifecycle

  1. Infection: Exploits, malicious downloads, or weak defaults on IoT/servers.
  2. Enrollment: Beacon to C2 via HTTP/S, DNS, or P2P.
  3. Tasking: Operators push jobs (DDoS, crypto mining, proxies).
  4. Monetization: Selling access, data theft, or extortion.

Blue‑Team Mitigations

  • Patch and change default credentials on routers/IoT.
  • Rate‑limit and add bot management/WAF rules at the edge.
  • Network egress filtering; monitor unusual DNS & traffic bursts.
  • Use CAPTCHA wisely; prefer behavior signals and verification flows.
