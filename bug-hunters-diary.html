A Bug Hunter‚Äôs Diary

Bug hunting is about curiosity and method. You look for the unexpected and reduce it to a minimal, reproducible proof of concept (PoC) that helps teams fix issues fast.

Flow

  1. Recon: Enumerate assets, tech stacks, and auth flows.
  2. Model: Map trust boundaries and risky assumptions.
  3. Exploit: Craft payloads (XSS, IDOR, SSRF, auth bypass).
  4. Report: Provide steps, impact, and remediation.

Tips

  • Automate the boring: wordlists, fuzzers, and param mining.
  • Keep notes; reproduce cleanly with screenshots and curl commands.
  • Focus on impact, not just novelty. Broken access control often wins.
